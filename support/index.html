Thanks for purchasing this Eco Friendly - A Complete template for organic , eco , environment and for nature.

Eco Friendly is a stunning, eye catching new HTML template, designed specially for organic farming business, green grocery store, agricultural company or basically everything related to eco-friendly lifestyle. This template was created after a detailed analysis of other theme-related websites and combines all the necessary features to meet the requirements of the pickiest customer. If you feel any issues please read this documentation and you will get to know everything, if you still need any help or you want me to customize it for you, you may send me an email at https://kodeforest.com/support.
